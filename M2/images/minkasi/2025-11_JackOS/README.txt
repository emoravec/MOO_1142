This folder contains two fits files, which are minkasi maps of MOO 1142. MOO_1142_PS_sub.fits is a point source subtracted map of MOO 1142. The point source was jointly fit with an r_s parameterized gNFW and subcluster on the X-ray peak. Just the PS was then subtracted from the time streams and the time streams mapmade. Note the PS was not re-fit, as fitting the PS alone tends to lead to sub-otimal removal. The PS parameters are in MOO_1142_PS_sub.yaml. MOO_1142_signal.fits is the pure signal map output by minkasi; nothing has been subtracted.

In 2026-04, Jack made the MOO_1142_snrmap.fits so that I could use it to plot contours in the paper. This was made with the 1 pass, 50 step signal map and the 0 pass, 25 step noise map. He added masking to the input images (from 2025-11 images) to somewhat reduce the effect of bowling.

I created a cutouts folder where I put cutouts centered on a by eye center that were 4.5'x4.5'.
