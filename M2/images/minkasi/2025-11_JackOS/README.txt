This folder contains two fits files, which are minkasi maps of MOOJ1142. MOO1142_PS.fits is a point source subtracted map of MOOJ1142. The point source was jointly fit with an r_s parameterized gNFW and subcluster on the X-ray peak. Just the PS was then subtracted from the time streams and the time streams mapmade. Note the PS was not re-fit, as fitting the PS alone tends to lead to sub-otimal removal. The PS parameters are in MOOJ1142_PS.yaml. MOO1142_signal.fits is the pure signal map output by minkasi; nothing has been subtracted.
