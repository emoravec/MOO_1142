For the RIGHT shock:
First, I made some regions with the M2 images so that I would have a reference of the size and shape of the shock - Moravec_M2_right_shock_outline_ggm2.reg is smaller and based on the GGM=2 image and Moravec_M2_right_shock_outline_ggm4.reg. See 01_MOO_1142_right_shock_compare_shock_regions.png for a comparison of the two regions. I decided to use the smaller region for reference in the rest of the analysis. To see what that looks like see 02_MOO_1142_M2_all_right_shock.png.

Second, I went to the X-ray to refine the region some more. Here is what the shock outline from M2 looks like on the X-ray images - 03_MOO_1142_X-ray_all_M2_right_shock.png. I created a large region that would encompass all the features of the shock outline from M2, but now encompass features from the X-ray data and create a slice that catches the variation from the central area of the cluster across the shock and give some padding on all sides - see 04_MOO_1142_right_shock_xray_small_large.png and 05_MOO_1142_right_shock_xray_large.png. I produced and intermediate region - Moravec_right_shock_xray.reg.

Third, I went back to the M2 images to make sure I was catching all the large scale variation in the M2 image and giving the region some padding too - 07_MOO_1142_right_shock_M2_large.png. 

Fourth, I went back to the X-ray images to make sure everything looked good, and it did (see 08_MOO_1142_right_shock_xray_large.png).

The final region I have for the right shock is Moravec_right_shock.reg.

For the LEFT shock I followed a similar procedure, starting with the M2 images, going to X-ray, then back to M2, but there was less refining in the last steps. 

I started out with M2 images again and made a smaller (Moravec_M2_left_shock_outline_ggm2.reg) and larger (Moravec_M2_left_shock_outline_ggm4.reg) region to outline the shock. See 01_MOO_1142_left_shock_compare_shock_regions.png for a comparison of the two regions. I decided to use the smaller region for reference in the rest of the analysis. To see what that looks like see 02_MOO_1142_M2_left_all_shock.png.

Second, I went to the X-ray to refine the region some more. Here is what the shock outline from M2 looks like on the X-ray images - 03_MOO_1142_X-ray_all_M2_left_shock.png. Again, I created a large region that would encompass all the features of interest - see 04_MOO_1142_left_shock_xray_small_large.png and 05_MOO_1142_left_shock_xray_large.png. I produced and intermediate region - Moravec_left_shock_xray.reg.

Third, I went back to the M2 images and made the region just a little smaller for a final region of Moravec_left_shock.reg - see 06_MOO_1142_left_shock_M2_large.png.
